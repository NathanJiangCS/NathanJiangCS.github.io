Things I've been working on

Directions to SMS

A simple solution for those times you are lost in the city without a data plan to turn to.
Just text 647 691 3881 with your location and your destination, and wait for a response with the appropriate directions.
Created by combining the power of Twilio's SMS API, Google Directions, and a Python Flask server.

Staysafewith.me

Sometimes, the fastest way to get home isn't what you're looking for. By utilizing Google's Direction API in conjunction with statistical crime data from the past 10 years, staysafewith.me is a web application that calculates the safest walking path through areas of Detroit.

Future plans to develop a cooperative mode are in the works!!
